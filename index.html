Scientists in Bulgaria may have figured out how to detect wormholes

Link to Article November 15, 2022

This well-written article explains how Bulgarian researchers discovered wormholes. There was a part in this article that explained what would happen if you were to fly into a wormhole, which would allow you to traverse far distances quickly. Wormholes and blackholes are difficult to distinguish from one another and operate in distinct ways. You need to examine the data from sophisticated telescopes and sensors to locate wormholes. The radiation intensity is used to quantify the data. Finding certain light manipulations is the latest technique for searching for wormholes. This article claims that finding wormholes is “like looking for a needle in a haystack,” which is quite intriguing to me. I learned more about wormholes after reading this article, which has a lot of useful information for individuals interested in space and wormholes.
